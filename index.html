The Artist

Yvonne Druppers

Born in Utrecht, Netherlands, and longtime resident of Far North Queensland, Australia, Yvonne creates evocative paintings that bridge the gap between perception and reality. Her work invites viewers to explore emotional landscapes as visual poetry.

Working primarily with oil on canvas, Yvonne creates layers of translucent color that seem to capture light from within. Her technique combines traditional methods with contemporary sensibilities, resulting in works that feel both timeless and thoroughly modern.
